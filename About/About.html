About Me

Fifi Xie is an MFA Candidate at Parson School of Design studying Design and Technology. Born and raised in Beijing, China, Fifi received her bachelor’s degrees (2017) from University of Washington in Philosophy and Industrial Design. She worked briefly as a footwear designer in 2017 and then started her Master Program in the fall of 2018.

Fifi's interests lies at the intersection of human and machine interactions and behaviors, with a focus on ethics. She started exploring and researching on inter-group conflict, collective and individual interests, and cognitive biases in her early graduate work. Fifi will be graduating from her program in May 2020.


You can find me at fifixiedesign@gmail.com
