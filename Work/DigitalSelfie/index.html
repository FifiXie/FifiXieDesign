Digital Selfie is a query of searching one's identity in a digitized world. We are unique in so many ways, yet we are so predictable. Do what I browse, purchase, stream and read online form an accurate narrative of who I am?

As data privacy rises as a bigger concern than ever, what can we learn from our own data? For this project, I requested and printed out all my personal data generated over the years by Facebook. It was shocking to see how many companies has requested my data from Facebook. Many of them I haven’t even heard of, let alone giving consent to let them having my data.
